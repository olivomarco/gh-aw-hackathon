## What you'll build

A daily workflow that analyses your test suite with expert-level depth and creates issues for concrete improvement opportunities. From `github/gh-aw` — 19 issues created, 13 led to merged PRs (**100% causal chain merge rate**). This is the *producer* half of a causal chain pattern.

## Concepts

- Domain expertise embedded directly in workflow prompts
- `safe-outputs: create-issue` (issue-creation only — no PRs)
- Causal chain pattern: one workflow creates issues, another acts on them
- Test quality analysis beyond simple coverage metrics
