# Timeline & Run-of-Show

## Format: Single Day (8 hours)

**Doors open:** 08:30  
**Kickoff:** 09:00  
**Close:** 17:00

---

## Pre-Event (Organizer Tasks)

| When | Task | Owner |
|------|------|-------|
| T-14 days | Participant registration opens | Events lead |
| T-7 days | Send pre-event email: GitHub account, Codespaces access, pre-read | Events lead |
| T-3 days | Coach briefing: format, rubric, hands-off policy | Lead |
| T-1 day | Test all challenges in fresh Codespace | QA |
| T-1 day | Verify repo permissions, team invites, Discord channels | Infra |
| Morning of | Confirm AV, Wi-Fi, power strips, coffee ☕ | Events lead |

---

## Day-Of Schedule

### Opening Block (08:30 – 09:45)

| Time | Duration | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 08:30 | 30 min | Doors open. Coffee. Networking. |
| 09:00 | 15 min | **Welcome & format briefing** — WTH rules, coaches, tracks, judging |
| 09:15 | 15 min | **Squad formation** — self-select or assigned (3–5 per squad) |
| 09:30 | 15 min | **Environment check** — everyone launches Codespace, runs smoke test |

### Track 1 — Hello, Agent (09:45 – 11:15)

| Time | Duration | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 09:45 | 5 min | Track 1 kickoff — coach introduces concepts |
| 09:50 | 80 min | Squads work challenges 1-01 through 1-04 (core challenges only; bonus challenges are stretch goals for fast-moving squads) |
| 11:10 | 5 min | Quick share: one squad demos their best workflow |

### Break (11:15 – 11:30)

### Track 2 — Repo Concierge (11:30 – 13:30)

| Time | Duration | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 11:30 | 10 min | Track 2 kickoff — coach demo: event triggers & slash commands |
| 11:40 | 100 min | Squads work challenges 2-01 through 2-05 (core challenges only; bonus challenges are stretch goals for fast-moving squads) |
| 13:20 | 10 min | Coach check-in: common blockers, pattern sharing |

### Lunch (13:30 – 14:15)

### Track 3 — Continuous Intelligence (14:15 – 16:00)

| Time | Duration | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 14:15 | 10 min | Track 3 kickoff — coach demo: workflow chaining & MCP |
| 14:25 | 85 min | Squads work challenges 3-01 through 3-05 (core challenges only; bonus challenges are stretch goals for fast-moving squads) |
| 15:50 | 10 min | Final commit deadline warning |

### Judging (16:00 – 16:45)

| Time | Duration | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 16:00 | 5 min | Pencils down. Push final commits. |
| 16:05 | 30 min | Squad demos (3 min each, ~8-10 squads) |
| 16:35 | 10 min | Judges deliberate |

### Closing (16:45 – 17:00)

| Time | Duration | Activity |
|------|----------|----------|
| 16:45 | 10 min | Awards: track winners, overall winner, special awards |
| 16:55 | 5 min | Retro: one word from each squad, feedback form link |
| 17:00 | — | Wrap. Photos. High fives. |

---

## Coach Touchpoints

Coaches should proactively check in at these moments:

- **09:50** — 10 min after Track 1 starts (are squads unblocked?)
- **11:40** — Track 2 start (new concepts, likely questions)
- **12:40** — Mid-Track 2 (are squads stuck on slash commands?)
- **14:25** — Track 3 start (hardest content, most support needed)
- **15:30** — 30 min before deadline (scope check: can they finish?)

---

## Contingency

- **Squads finishing early:** Point them to bonus challenges or next track
- **Squads stuck:** Coach escalation → 5-min unblock hint, not solution
- **Infrastructure failure:** Fallback to local devcontainers (Docker required)
- **Time overrun:** Cut Track 3 to challenges 3-01 and 3-02 only; judging stays fixed
